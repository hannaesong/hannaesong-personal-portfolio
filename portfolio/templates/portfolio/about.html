About

Yours truly and this website

I am Hanna Song, the Jack-of-all-Trades, the Perculiar Crack in the System, the Old Soul, Wandering Dove, and Tentative Scribe.

This is a webiste that I made to, first of all, learn website development and, second, to showcase my thoughts, hobbies, and story. I honestly haven't been doing a good job at keeping this up to date. Feel free to bully me into updating this.

You may be here because you asked me about my childhood or where I lived before now. Well, your answer is in the Timeline.

I think that I am something of a writer–I try to be anyway–which is why I've included a blog. I'll be writing short essays, thoughts, and maybe stories on what I hope will be a monthly basis. Or not.

Back in the day–in the days of Pakistan to Nepal–I read quite a bit. Though I have slacked considerably since then, I still like to keep record of the books I have read recently; so any recent reads may end up here on the website as well.

All of the photos featured on this website are either old family photos or ones taken by my sister, Irang.
